Vietnamese Old-World Charm with Direct Beach Access to the East Sea’s Turquoise Waters

Phan Thiet, Vietnam

Discover a charming haven set between the East Sea’s turquoise waters and Mui Ne’s golden dunes. A luxury beachfront resort nestled in lush foliage, relaxation will wash over you in one of two palm-fringed infinity pools, overlooking the ocean mere steps away. Seek serenity at the resort’s onsite spa, a tranquil sanctuary where Asian healing traditions interweave into holistic wellness journeys. Wine and dine at three exquisite restaurants and bars or immerse yourself in traditional Vietnamese cuisine in a step-by-step cooking class. Though it’s tempting to stay at the resort, Mui Ne’s Fairy Stream - a mesmerizing wonderland of natural waters and rock formations - is well worth the adventure. Take day trips to the famous White and Red Sand Dunes that are perfect for an exciting adventure. When night falls, don’t miss the sunset at the historic Mui Ne Fishing Village.
